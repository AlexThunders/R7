Instruction

You can track your tasks during a month by putting amount of hours, dedicated to a specific task, to a calendar.

  • Type hours in Time column
  • Mark check if you want to save it
  • Every check mark adds extra green tone to a day
  • By default date is today
  • Click Change date to indicate specific date
  • Click Save button
  • All tasks per one day are checked - the day is covered in Lilac
